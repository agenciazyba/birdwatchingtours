We are delighted to have you join us for this unforgettable adventure. Upon your arrival at Guarulhos International Airport, our team will be there to warmly welcome you and assist with your car transfer. From the moment you land, we’ll ensure your comfort and peace of mind as we begin this remarkable journey together. Get ready to immerse yourself in the breathtaking beauty and unparalleled biodiversity of the Mata Atlântica. This is just the beginning of an incredible experience!

Today marks your first full day at Patizal and your introduction to the incredible Mata Atlântica forest—the most biodiverse forest in Brazil. Situated in a unique transition zone between humid and less humid habitats, this area is renowned for the astonishing variety and abundance of wildlife you’ll encounter. It’s not uncommon to spot over 100 bird species in a single day at this remarkable location! As the day transitions to night, your guide will take you on an exciting nocturnal exploration. Together, you’ll search for elusive night birds, as well as fascinating amphibians and reptiles that come to life after dark. Get ready for an unforgettable experience filled with sights and sounds that highlight the wonders of this extraordinary ecosystem!
