After a day of discovery and exploration, we will return to the hotel to relax and reflect on the experience.

            We will continue our journey to Novo Airão, a city approximately 200 kilometers from Manaus and the gateway to Anavilhanas National Park—one of the largest freshwater archipelagos in the world. Upon reaching Novo Airão, we will check into our lodge before embarking on an afternoon birding excursion.               During our stay in Novo Airão, we will explore not only Anavilhanas National Park but also Jaú National Park, both of which are renowned for their exceptional biodiversity. These protected areas offer visitors unparalleled opportunities to observe a wide array of species in their natural habitats. The region boasts a unique landscape, shaped by its distinct biome, and is home to a high degree of endemism, ensuring an unforgettable experience for nature enthusiasts. Throughout our stay, we will monitor weather conditions to determine the optimal times for boat tours to the nearby islands, maximizing the chances of observing the area’s diverse wildlife.
