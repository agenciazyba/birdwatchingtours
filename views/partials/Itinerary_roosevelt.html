    After breakfast, we will head to the city of Apuí, located on the banks of the Roosevelt River. The journey, approximately 7 hours long, will take us along the iconic Transamazonian Highway, one of the most famous roads in the Amazon. Along the way, we will make several strategic stops to observe the rich local wildlife and cross the Madeira River by Ferry, an event by itslef, crossing one of the region's most important waterways. Our arrival at the lodge is expected in the afternoon, allowing time to rest and prepare for the upcoming activities. Our guide will be happy to take anyone to find the night critters.
